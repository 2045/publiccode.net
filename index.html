Mission

We help develop public digital infrastructure that is inclusive, usable, adaptive and sustainable.

We are focused on creating a viable future for cities and civic operating systems that are highly participatory and drive societal engagement.

We communicate, prototype, and implement this future by researching and developing applications, technologies and policy.
